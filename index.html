About Me

Hello, I'm Dylan Forster, a promising web developer/programmer. I'm currently studying full stack development and it's now become my passion. I've always had a fascination with technology and how it can be used to bring creative solutions to life through the power of code.

Professionally, I've worked through various customer service jobs and have developed strong communication and problem-solving skills that I believe are essential in the tech industry.
